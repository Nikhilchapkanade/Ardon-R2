# Code of Conduct

Ardon-R2 is a small, open project. The rules here are short and mean
what they say — no external document to chase.

## The standard

Everyone taking part in Ardon-R2's repository, issues, discussions,
and pull requests is expected to:

- Be respectful and assume good faith.
- Keep criticism technical — about code, ideas, and behaviour, never
  about a person's identity or background.
- Welcome newcomers and answer questions patiently.
- Accept that contributions are judged on technical merit alone.

The following are not acceptable: harassment, personal attacks,
discriminatory or demeaning remarks, deliberate intimidation, sharing
others' private information, or sustained disruption of discussion.

## Scope

This applies in every project space (GitHub repository, Issues,
Discussions, pull requests) and whenever someone is representing the
project in public.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, contact the
maintainer privately:

- **Devendra Tandale** — direct message on GitHub (**@devendratandle**),
  or use the private contact path in `SECURITY.md`.

Reports are handled as promptly and fairly as a single maintainer can,
and the privacy of anyone reporting is respected.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove comments, reject contributions, or — for
serious or repeated violations — ban a participant from project spaces.
As a one-person project this is best-effort, applied in good faith and
proportionally.
