### Why AGPL v3?

R2 is licensed under AGPL v3 because we believe in balancing openness
with sustainability:

- **Free for personal use** — students, researchers, consultants can
  use R2 without any cost, forever.
- **Free for consulting work** — if you use R2 to analyze data for
  clients, that's free.
- **AGPL for commercial embedding** — if you build R2 into a commercial
  product or cloud service, the AGPL requires you to share your
  modifications. This protects the community's work.

Nothing is free in this world — everything has a price. But money alone
is not what matters. What matters is that the people who dedicate their
lives to building R2 — the core team, the contributors, the community —
can sustain themselves while doing meaningful work.

We chose AGPL v3 because:
1. It keeps R2 open and accessible to everyone
2. It ensures commercial users contribute back
3. It allows the core team to offer commercial licenses when appropriate
4. It maintains transparency, which builds trust

**Trust through transparency. Sustainability through fairness.**
